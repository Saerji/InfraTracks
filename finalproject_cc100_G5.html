Our Advocacy

Be vigilant, watch your surroundings and be updated.

InfraTrack is a citizen-led advocacy initiative dedicated to promoting accountability, transparency, and ethical governance in public infrastructure projects. We believe that every road, bridge, school, hospital, and government-funded project must serve the people—efficiently, honestly, and sustainably.
Our mission is simple: empower communities to see where their taxes go. By tracking infrastructure from planning to completion, we ensure that no project remains hidden, mismanaged, or misleading.
