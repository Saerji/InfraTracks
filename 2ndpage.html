Build

Building of infrastructures are a crucial way for the infrastructure's integrity and longevity. Planning, transparent budgeting, and the use of quality materials all play a crucial role to achieve such quality infrastructures. Building encompasses the government agencies' wants to provide quality life for its civillians. Ensuring that this process has essential accountability and authenticity is a role for every individual to demand, no matter what.

Check

Checking is a way for civillians to ensure the building of governmental infrastructures is thoroughly planned and contemplated by the government agencies. Checking the different factors ensures a quality life equal to those what normal civillians give to the government. Checking the authenticity over infrastructures is an easy thing but an essential action in order to maintain the integrity of those what we use and ensures security, safety and fairness for everyone.
So be sure to always check.

REPORT

Reporting is a significant action to take as the one who will utilize the infrastructures that are being built. Though it takes courage to do, this action paves the way for the right development to occur, ensuring the wrong to be right, and to fix what is destroyed. InfraTrack allows its user to submit a report about infrastructure-related problems, it ensures that your information is kept with utmost confidentiality and the actions that are taken will always takes place, for you to witness it all.

About FOI

Additionally, FOI (Freedom of Information) is an accessible website that utilizes technology to be used by normal individuals to strengthen the the checking of infrastructure building in the Philippines. FOI.gov.ph is the official online portal of the Philippines’ Freedom of Information Program, providing a centralized and streamlined platform where citizens can request government documents, access publicly available data, and explore agency resources in line with Executive Order No. 2, s. 2016—promoting transparency, accountability, and informed public participation.
